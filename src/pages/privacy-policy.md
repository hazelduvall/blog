## Analytics

### What personal data we collect and why we collect it

I collect basic information about visitors via CloudFront logs, like IP address
and referrer header. This information is retained for a maximum of 1 month
before being permanently deleted. I keep it in order to see roughly how many
people (and bots) visit this site.
